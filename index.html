About Me

Bio

Hi, I'm Layla, and I'm a Computer Science graduate from James Madison University.

I started programming in fifth grade through the robotics club at my elementary school. Being introduced to technology at such a young age inspired me to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Outside of coding, I like music, cars, Marvel Rivals, and Minecraft (which you can probably tell from the theme of this website). I enjoy learning about software development, exploring new technologies, and building things that challenge me.

More than anything, I want to use technology to help people and bring communities together. Whether that's through software, outreach, education, or collaborative projects, I believe technology is at its best when it connects people and makes a positive impact.
